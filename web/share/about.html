Muse

A free, local-first universal file viewer and AI-organized asset library for macOS. Your files stay on your Mac — Muse collects no data.

Muse can optionally publish a collection of images as a shareable web link through your own Google Drive. That feature is opt-in and only runs when you choose to use it; the developer never receives your files or any data about them.

Muse is free, open-source software.
